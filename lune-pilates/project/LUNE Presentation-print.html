Highlights
Six features that make LUNE feel effortless

Bilingual by default

Thai-first with an instant TH · EN toggle on every screen — comfortable for members and staff alike.

Credits & expiry, up front

Hours remaining and the validity date are the hero — time-limited packages never expire by surprise.

Reformer-lane availability

Each of the three reformers shows as a lane — booked or open — with instructor choice on Private, Duo & Trio.

Fair waitlists

Full classes never auto-book. A freed spot notifies the next member, who confirms within a short window.

House-number sharing

Members share credits across a household by house number — a benefit guests don't get, shown clearly in both apps.

PromptPay & LINE

Pay by the QR flow Thai members trust, with booking confirmations and reminders delivered through LINE.

05
Designed-in care
The details that prevent disputes

5-hour cutoff, made obvious

Free cancel or reschedule up to five hours before class. Inside the window, the app shows exactly what a cancellation costs — one credit — before you confirm.

Waitlist = notify + confirm

Full classes never auto-book. When a spot opens, the member is notified and has a short window to confirm and claim it — fair and transparent.

House-number sharing

Membership ties to a house number so credits can be shared within a household — a benefit guests don't get, surfaced clearly in both apps.

Calm UX is not just how it looks — it's removing the moments where a member could feel confused or treated unfairly.
